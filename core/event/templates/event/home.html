About The Event

The Worship Concert and Gospel Competitions, organized by Kingdom First Worldwide Ministries and hosted by Voices of Worship and Praise, will take place on 25th April 2026 from 10:00 AM at Odiado Rehabilitation Hall. The event will bring together gospel artists and worship teams for a day of praise, worship, and musical competitions, with winners receiving awards.
